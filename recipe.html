Delicious Pancakes

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 egg2 tablespoons melted butter

Instructions

  1. In a bowl,mic flour,sugar,baking powder and salt
  2. In another bowl,beat egg with milk and added butter
  3. Combine wet and dry ingredients and mix until smooth
  4. Heat a skillet and pour in batter to form pancakes
  5. Cook until bubbles form,then flip and cook until golden
Enjoy your homemade pancakes
